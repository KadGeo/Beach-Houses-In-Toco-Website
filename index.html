Experience authentic Caribbean living at our beach houses in Toco, Trinidad's pristine northeastern village. Nestled where the Caribbean Sea meets the Atlantic Ocean, Toco offers dramatic coastal views, secluded beaches like Salybia Bay, and the famous Galera Point lighthouse. Wake up to breathtaking ocean vistas, explore hidden coves and waterfalls, and immerse yourself in genuine coastal accommodation that connects you with Trinidad's most untouched natural paradise.

About

Welcome to Toco Beach Houses, your premier booking agency for northeastern Trinidad beach houses and coastal accommodation in the village of Toco. For over a decade, we've connected visitors with perfect beachfront rentals while supporting the local communities of this spectacular coastal region.

Our work with local property owners provide authentic Caribbean hospitality through guest houses and beach resorts in the community. By booking with us, you support local families and contribute to the economic vitality of this coastal paradise, while enjoying affordable stays just steps away from pristine beaches and rainforest trails.
