## Image Sourcing Options

### Option 1: Professional Photography
- Hire local Trinidad photographer
- Visit Toco for photoshoot
- Capture authentic locations: Salybia Bay, Galera Point, local beach houses
- **Pros:** Authentic, unique, perfect for brand
- **Cons:** More expensive, requires planning

### Option 2: Stock Photography
- Search terms: "Trinidad beach", "Caribbean coastline", "tropical beach house"
- Sources: Unsplash, Pexels, Shutterstock
- **Pros:** Quick, affordable
- **Cons:** Less authentic, may not show exact locations

### Option 3: Drone/Aerial Photography
- Toco's dramatic coastline works beautifully from above
- Capture the meeting of two seas
- **Pros:** Stunning, unique perspective
- **Cons:** Requires drone operator

### Option 4: Mix Approaches
- Use stock for initial launch
- Plan professional shoot for v2
- Replace images gradually

3. Keep originals as backups
