## Content Strategy - Toco Unique Selling Points

### Geographic Highlights
1. **Location**: Northeastern tip where Caribbean Sea meets Atlantic Ocean
2. **Beaches**: Salybia Bay (calm, protected), Balandra Bay, secluded coves
3. **Landmarks**: Galera Point lighthouse, Paria Falls
4. **Nearby**: Grand Riviere (turtle nesting)

### Activities & Attractions
- Hiking to Paria Falls through rainforest
- Galera Point lighthouse visits
- Leatherback turtle watching (March-August)
- Beach hopping to multiple pristine beaches
- Bird watching and photography
- Fishing (charter or shore)
- Dramatic sunrise photography

### Practical Information
- **Nearest Major Town**: Sangre Grande (30-40 minutes)
- **Road Conditions**: Some unpaved roads, 4WD may be recommended
- **Cell Service**: Spotty in some areas - digital detox appeal
- **Best Season**: Dry season (January-May) for calmer seas

### Target Audience Shifts
- **Mayaro**: Families, weekend beach limes, fishing culture
- **Toco**: Nature lovers, adventurers, eco-tourists, photographers, peace seekers
